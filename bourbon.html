Rare Bourbon Finder.

Objective: Identify stock arrival of rare bourbons at local retail locations.

Background

As any bourbon fan already knows: some of the best tasting bourbons are also the hardest to come by. The objective of this bourbon finder is to identify the time and location when rare bourbons hit the shelves in the the great state of North Carolina. The Rare Bourbon Finder is a Python script that navigates NC state liquor inventories, scrapes inventory data, and send notifications when that sweet bottle of bourbon is in stock and where to grab it.

This script was originally written with Blanton's in mind, but can be used for any bourbon distributed by North Carolina.
