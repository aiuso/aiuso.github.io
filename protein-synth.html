Protein Synthesis Question Generator.

Objective: Randomly generate questions for students to practice transcribing DNA to RNA and translating RNA into peptides.

Background

Protein synthesis is a cellular process of turning information stored in genetic material (DNA) into a functional molecule that can carry out cellular functions. However, proteins cannot be made directly from DNA, the DNA must first be copied into a ‘protein readable’ type of genetic material called RNA.

The process of transcribing DNA to RNA and translating RNA into protein, is a fundamental process in biology known as protein synthesis. This question generator will randomly generate a sequence of DNA that students will have to transcribe into RNA. Then using a codon chart, they will have to determined the sequence of amino acids that would be arranged from that RNA.
